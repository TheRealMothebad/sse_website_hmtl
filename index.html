Who We Are

MTU Supermileage Systems is a student-run organization focused on the design, manufacturing, and testing of a single seat vehicle to achieve the greatest fuel economy possible.

Our Competition

Shell Eco-Marathon

Hosted at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway every year in April. The Shell Eco-Marathon Competition provides opportunities for teams around the world to compete and push the boundaries of efficiency. Allowing the development of advanced powertrains in Battery Electric, Internal Combustion Engine, Hydrogen Fuel Cell, and much more.
